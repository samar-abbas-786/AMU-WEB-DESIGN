Centre for Skill Development and Career Planning

About Us

Established in 1984 The Centre for Skill Development and Career Planning was initially established to impart vocational skill training to the female students of A.M.U. and later extended to ex-students, external students and home makers. The Centre for Skill Development and Career Planning was created to help women realize their potential and to make them independent. The Centre has adopted the scheme of Earn While You Learn since the last 37 years. This scheme was launched to motivate the students and its successful implementation has not only created enthusiasm about joining a skill programme but has also developed awareness and motivation among female students for their achievements and success. The Centre provides opportunity to underprivileged women who can join these skill development courses. The centre endeavours to provide knowledge and skill to underpriviledged women by organizing various Crash Courses in computer literacy, Textile Designing, Beauty Treatment, Flower Making and English Fluency Development. Workshops, Orientation and Awareness Programmes are conducted regularly at the centre. This centre is a growing organization working steadily in the direction of women’s empowerment.
